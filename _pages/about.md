I am an undergraduate student in [Computer Science and Technology (Zhiyuan Honors Program, JOHN Class)](https://en.zhiyuan.sjtu.edu.cn/) at [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://en.sjtu.edu.cn/), expected to graduate in June 2026.

## Research Interests

I am interested in code-based cryptography and zero-knowledge proof systems, including code-based polynomial commitments, prover-efficient and distributed proving protocols, and communication-efficient constructions for practical deployment.
